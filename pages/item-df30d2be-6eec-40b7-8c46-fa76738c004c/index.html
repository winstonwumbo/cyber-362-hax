How to Access Splunk

The College of IST hosts its own instance of Splunk at the address splunk.ist.psu.edu. However, this IST instance is restricted and can only be accessed from the Penn State networks psu or eduroam to authenticate. Students may simply visit splunk.ist.psu.edu from the computer labs or their own devices while on-campus.
